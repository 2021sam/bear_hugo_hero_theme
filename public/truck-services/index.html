BEAR Collison Center provides comprehensive repair solutions for all types of trucks and heavy-duty construction equipment. Our auto body shop offers a full suite of services tailored to meet the needs of truckers and fleet operators.

At our facility, we understand the critical importance of minimizing downtime and ensuring that your vehicles remain in optimal condition. Our expert technicians are trained to handle a variety of repairs including collision and frame repairs, as well as specialized work on composites, aluminum, and steel. We are equipped to service Class A trucks, medium duty trucks, truck bodies, pickups, vans and cars.

Services include:

Class A Truck and Bus Service

  • Collision repairs
  • Frame repairs
  • Composites, aluminum and steel repairs
  • Painting and re-imaging

Medium Duty Trucks and Truck Bodies

  • Repair damaged service bodies
  • Repair damaged dry freight bodies
  • Repair reefer bodies
  • Repair high cube bodies
  • Lift-gate repairs

Pickups, Vans and Cars

Heavy Duty Construction Equipment
